GMH is a growing asset-based trucking company and third-party logistics provider with warehousing. Founded 26 years ago in Vermont, we now run terminals coast to coast — servicing FTL, LTL, and Liquid Tanker loads for everyone from large multi-national shippers to small local businesses.

We offer a wide range of work: local, regional, OTR, and dedicated accounts — just enough variety to keep things interesting!

Why GMH?

🏛️ We're Stable

26 years in business means we've operated through recessions, market swings, and everything in between. You're not joining a startup — you're joining a company with proven staying power.

📈 We're Growing

Our customer base is expanding faster than we can service it. We're actively looking for qualified drivers because the freight is there — we just need good people to move it.

💰 We Want You to Succeed

GMH pays a percentage of revenue rather than a flat rate per mile. When freight rates go up, your paycheck goes up. We're incentivized to put you on quality loads — it's a true partnership.

Pay Structure

GMH pays a percentage of revenue (less fuel) — not a flat rate per mile.
On the whole, drivers earn significantly more through a percentage revenue model. You share in a strengthening market: when rates rise, you earn more. GMH is a service focused carrier and our rates reflect that.
